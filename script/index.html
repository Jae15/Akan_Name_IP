Find Your Akan Name

Akan names have their origin in Ghanian culture.

In Ghana, children get their first name according to the respective day in the week they were born.

The following is an application that utilizes a user's date of birth to calculate the day of the week on which they were born and then allocates them an Akan name that corresponds to their respective genders.
